## Farm Animals

Cow – Classic, recognizable, and great for adding big, fun spots.

Pig – Excellent for simple, round shapes and a curly tail that kids love to color.

Sheep – Perfect for texture! Kids can practice coloring fluffy, cloud-like wool.

Chicken (Rooster or Hen) – Great for introducing bright colors like reds, oranges, and yellows on the comb and feathers.

Horse – Offers a nice long mane and tail for a bit more detail.

Duck – Simple, clean lines and a big, friendly beak make this an easy favorite for younger children.

Goat – A quirky addition with fun floppy ears and a little beard to color.

Donkey – Famous for big, expressive ears and a cute, friendly face.

Alpaca (or Llama) – Adds a wonderfully goofy, long-necked, and super-shaggy look to the mix.

Barn Cat – A familiar, cozy animal that kids can personalize to look like their own pets.
