№259 Local-warp registration check (the shimmer suspect)

creatures/dragon-wide

left: shipped light fill, 480x480 crop of the dragon's face at native 1x (1536x1024 asset pixels); right: the center 240x240 of that same crop upscaled 2x with sharp's default lanczos3 kernel — the pixel density a 3x tablet in landscape would ask of this asset.

Same face region of dragon-wide.light.webp at native scale vs ~2x lanczos upscale. At 1x (left) the eye outlines, brow strokes, and nostrils are crisp; at 2x (right) every line edge goes soft and the punched line-adjacent fill detail smears — look at the halo around the eye rims, the brow strokes, and the mouth edge. Evidence supports the issue's proposed fixes (measure real rendered size on target devices, then regenerate higher-res or upscale raws + re-punch).
